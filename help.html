Navigating the Dashboard

The dashboard has two main pages. The index.html file contains interactive charts and key performance indicators. Use the View Data Tables button at the top to switch views. The data-tables.html page provides sortable tables with export options. Use its tabs to choose datasets and the CSV/PDF buttons to download data.

Chart Guide

Hover over each chart to see exact values. Important visualizations include:

  • Monthly Revenue & Utilization Trend – bars show monthly net revenue while the line tracks machine utilization ((cycles completed / available cycles) × 100).
  • Weekly Performance Comparison – current week vs. last week and the 4‑week average. Percentage change uses ((current - previous) / previous) × 100.
  • Revenue Distribution by Day – daily revenue bars and a 4‑week average line reveal strong and weak days.
  • Customer Segment Charts – see how different groups contribute to revenue. Average value per customer equals total revenue ÷ number of customers.
  • Marketing ROI Dashboard – ROI is (revenue from campaign - cost) ÷ cost. Values above zero indicate profit.

Metric Definitions

  • Net Revenue = total revenue − refunds and adjustments.
  • Utilization (%) = (completed cycles ÷ maximum possible cycles) × 100.
  • Revenue Efficiency = net revenue ÷ utilization %.
  • Growth Rate = ((current − reference) ÷ reference) × 100.
  • Correlation Coefficient – values near ±1 indicate a strong relationship.
