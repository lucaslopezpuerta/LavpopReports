Strategic Recommendations

Launch Off-Peak Hour Discount Campaign

Utilization remains low during most off-peak hours (particularly 8 AM–9 AM and 6 PM–9 PM). Use the POS system to apply a temporary 10–15% discount during these hours to attract more usage and spread demand into underused times, increasing both utilization and overall revenue.

Reactivate Lapsed Customers with WhatsApp Coupon Campaign

With 78.7% of customers currently lapsed, implement a reactivation campaign via WhatsApp offering a limited-time wash or dry cycle discount for those inactive more than 30 days. Segment messaging based on visit history and highlight the cashback benefit to boost return likelihood and monthly revenue.

Increase Basket Size by Bundling Bags with Multi-Cycle Offers

Average cycles per visit hover around 1.07, with even Champions only reaching 1.16. Create a bundled offer like “Buy 3 cycles and get a laundry bag free” to encourage higher spending per visit, utilize underused dryer capacity, and increase ancillary product sales.
