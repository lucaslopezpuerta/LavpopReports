Strategic Recommendations

Launch Off-Peak Hour Discounts to Maximize Machine Use

Utilization is still low at just 18%, with a significant gap between peak and off-peak hours. Use MaxPan's time-based pricing feature to offer a 10–15% discount during underused hours (like 8–9 AM, 12–2 PM, and after 6 PM) to increase cycles and drive more foot traffic throughout the day.

Target Lapsed Customers with WhatsApp Credit Bonus Campaign

Since 78.8% of customers are inactive and only 21.2% return within 90 days, send exclusive WhatsApp messages offering a limited-time BRL 10 bonus credit for recharging BRL 50 or more. This can re-engage dormant users and increase upfront revenue via credit prepayments.

Bundle Wash+Dry Services with Value-Added Offers

Dryer usage is lagging behind washers (12% vs. 28% utilization). Create attractive bundles such as “Wash+Dry Combo” with a 5% discount or a free branded laundry bag (worth BRL 17.90) after three full combos. This encourages customers to use both services and increases number of dryer cycles.
