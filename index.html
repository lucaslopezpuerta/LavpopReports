Optimize Hourly Pricing During Peak Times

Given the observed peak times at 10 AM, 11 AM, 1 PM, 3 PM, and 6 PM, implement dynamic pricing to increase cycle costs slightly during these times by 5-10%. This strategy leverages high demand periods to boost net revenue and can be easily managed through the POS system MaxPan which allows automatic price adjustments.

Enhanced Marketing to 'Lapsed' and 'Potential' Customer Segments

With 79.3% of customers identified as lapsed, create targeted WhatsApp campaigns offering special discounts or loyalty rewards to encourage return visits. Tailor messages to show personalized promotions such as a 10% increase in cashback rates or a special rate for bulk cycle purchases to 'Potential' customers to foster their transition into more engaged segments like 'Loyal' or even 'Champion'.
