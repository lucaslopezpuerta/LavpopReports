Strategic Recommendations

Target Lapsed Customers with WhatsApp Coupon Campaign

With 78.8% of your customers currently lapsed and a low return rate within 30-90 days, launching a limited-time WhatsApp campaign offering a 10% washing discount coupon can re-engage this segment. Target "Potential" and "New" RFM segments with tailored messages to boost repeat cycles and lift revenue during underutilized hours.

Activate Smart Time-Based Pricing During Off-Peak Hours

Utilization is stagnating around 17–18%, indicating underused capacity. Leverage MaxPan’s automatic pricing feature to offer BRL 2.00 discounts on wash cycles between 9 AM–12 PM and 2 PM–4 PM, incentivizing use during consistent off-peak hours and increasing cycle volume without compromising peak profits.

Bundle Drying and Laundry Bag in a “Ready-to-Go” Promo

Dryer utilization lags significantly behind washers (12% vs. 28%). Create a combo deal offering a drying cycle and the laundry bag together at a 10% discount. Promote this bundle in-store and via WhatsApp to encourage full-service usage and increase revenue per customer visit.
