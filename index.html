Strategic Recommendations

Implement Dynamic Off-Peak Discounts During Low Utilization Hours

Utilization remains low at 18% with a clear drop during off-peak hours like 8 AM, 9 AM, and post-7 PM. Activate MaxPan's price adjustment feature to offer a 10-15% discount during these time slots to incentivize usage without affecting peak-hour revenue, increasing machine productivity throughout the day.

Reactivate Lapsed Customers with Targeted WhatsApp Cashback Campaigns

With 78.7% of customers currently inactive, launching a time-limited 15% cashback promo (via WhatsApp) toward lapsed customers can drive reactivation. Focus particularly on the “Potential” and “New” segments to boost short-term volume and reactivate up to 21% of customers within 90 days.

Bundle Service Cycles with the Wash Bag to Drive Transaction Value

The wash bag is currently priced the same as one cycle, but not being positioned for volume growth. Offer a bundled deal (e.g., 2 cycles + bag for BRL 49.90) to upsell during peak hours and appeal to new or loyal segments—enhancing perceived value and increasing average ticket size.
