Strategic Recommendations

Launch Off-Peak Hour Discounts to Boost Utilization

With utilization steady at 18% and most activity concentrated during specific peak hours (11 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM, 7 PM), use MaxPan’s price scheduling to offer 10–15% discounts during identified off-peak hours (e.g., 8–10 AM, 2–5 PM, 8–11 PM). This will encourage visits during quieter periods, better distribute machine usage, and unlock capacity without additional investment.

Increase Average Basket Size with Bundled Promotions

With the average customer using only 1.08 cycles per visit, incentivize multi-cycle visits by offering promotions like "Get 1 dry cycle at 50% off when buying a wash + dry combo." This directly targets increasing per-visit spend and is especially effective among "Loyal" and "Potential" customers, whose cycle usage is still below Champions.

Re-Engage Lapsed Customers via WhatsApp Flash Campaigns

Since 78.4% of customers are lapsed and only 10.1% return within 30 days, use WhatsApp to trigger flash promotions—like "Come back this weekend and get 20% off your next wash." Focus messaging on reactivation and create urgency to drive short-term re-engagement and improve long-term retention.
