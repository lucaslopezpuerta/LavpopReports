Strategic Recommendations

Launch Off-Peak Hour Discounts to Boost Utilization

Utilization is low during off-peak hours (e.g. 14h, 16h, 21h), and MaxPan allows time-based pricing. Introduce a 10-15% discount during these times to drive volume and better distribute demand throughout the day, compensating for underused hours and increasing revenue per machine.

Create a WhatsApp Loyalty Coupon for Lapsed Customers

With 79.2% of customers lapsed and only 20.8% likely to return within 90 days, send targeted WhatsApp messages offering an exclusive coupon (e.g. 15% off one wash or dry) to re-engage lapsed users. Tailor message copy per RFM segments (New, Potential, Loyal) to increase reactivations and repeat cycles.

Upsell with Bundled Bag + Cycle Promotions

The branded bag, priced the same as a single cycle, offers upsell potential. Promote a limited-time bundle via WhatsApp—buy one wash + bag for BRL 31.90 instead of BRL 35.80—to increase average transaction value while reinforcing brand visibility and convenience for repeat usage.
