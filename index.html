Data-driven insights and actionable recommendations

**1. Launch Off-Peak Hour Discounts to Boost Utilization** Current machine utilization is at 15%, with significantly lower activity during off-peak hours (especially between 12 PM and 2 PM, and after 6 PM). Use MaxPan’s automatic time-based pricing to offer 10-15% discounts during these off-peak hours to attract more customers when machines are typically idle, increasing overall revenue without additional capacity investment. **2. Reactivate Lapsed Customers with Targeted WhatsApp Campaigns** With 76.8% of customers currently lapsed and a low 23.2% 90-day return likelihood, use WhatsApp to send re-engagement messages featuring time-limited coupons (e.g., 10% off one cycle valid for 3 days). Personalize messages based on previous usage and offer bundles (e.g., 2 cycles + free bag) to incentivize return visits. **3. Increase Visit Frequency in Loyal Segments with Tiered Credit Bonuses** Loyal and Champion customers already have higher visit rates and cycle usage. Encourage more frequent visits by offering tiered credit bonuses — for example, “Buy BRL 100 in credit and get BRL 10 extra.” Promote this directly via WhatsApp to active users, leveraging their higher likelihood of engagement without relying on discounts alone.
