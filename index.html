Strategic Recommendations

Boost Off-Peak Usage with Targeted Time-Based Discounts

Utilization rates are still low (18%), and off-peak hours represent a large portion of operating time. Use MaxPan’s pricing control to offer a 10–15% discount on wash and dry cycles during consistently underutilized hours like 8 AM, 9 AM, and 8–9 PM. Announce these promotions via WhatsApp to increase throughput and spread demand more evenly throughout the day.

Re-Engage Lapsed Customers with WhatsApp-Centric Loyalty Promotions

With 78.8% of your customer base lapsed and only 10.5% likely to return in 30 days, launch a reactivation campaign via WhatsApp offering a time-limited 20% discount coupon for the next visit. Include dynamic messaging tailored to past visit frequency and encourage credit pre-purchase to increase retention and promote recurring visits.

Upsell Multi-Cycle Visits to High-Frequency Segments

Champion and Loyal customers already visit frequently but perform only 1.1–1.25 cycles per visit. Incentivize them to perform at least one additional cycle by bundling services (e.g., “2 cycles for BRL 32”) and promoting this offer during their preferred hours through WhatsApp. Adding just one more cycle increases your weekly revenue potential by over 15% among your most valuable segment.
