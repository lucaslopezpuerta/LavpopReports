Strategic Recommendations

Introduce Off-Peak Hour Discounts to Boost Machine Utilization

Utilization remains low at 18%, with off-peak hours comprising most of the operating day. Implement a small price reduction (e.g., 10%) for cycles between 8–10 AM and 2–5 PM to incentivize usage when machines are idle. This can be easily managed via MaxPan’s time-based price adjustment feature and can help spread demand more evenly throughout the day.

Launch Limited-Time Reactivation Offers for Lapsed Customers

With over 79% of Lavpop’s customers lapsed and only 10.4% returning within 30 days, targeted WhatsApp campaigns with exclusive discount codes or 2-cycles-for-1 promos can reactivate dormant users. Focus on previous high-frequency customers in the Potential and Loyal segments to drive volume during off-peak hours.

Bundle Wash & Dry Cycles with Value-Added Products

Given the disparity in washer (28%) vs. dryer (12%) utilization, promote washer and dryer combo packages that include a free laundry bag or bonus cashback. This not only increases average revenue per customer but also improves dryer utilization and promotes accessory sales, all within your existing pricing and POS capabilities.
